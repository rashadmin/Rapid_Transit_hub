Welcome to Rapid Care Hub

Rapid Care Hub is your prototype emergency response app, giving you a sneak peek into the future of immediate medical assistance.

How it works:

  • Share your situation and receive guidance from our AI helper.
  • Pretend hospital coordination (for now) to connect you with nearby hospitals.
  • Stay informed about the progress of your request (sort of).

Why give it a try?

  • Explore the exciting possibilities of future emergency assistance apps.
  • Have fun in this prototype playground.
  • Learn and explore the potential of technology in emergencies.

A Little Reminder: While Rapid Care Hub is intriguing, it's crucial to remember that it's just a prototype. In real emergencies, always seek immediate medical assistance from professionals or hospitals.
